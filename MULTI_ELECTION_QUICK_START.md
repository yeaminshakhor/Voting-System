## For Voters üó≥Ô∏è

### What You Need to Know

#### If Policy is: ‚úÖ ALLOW Multi-Election Voting
- You can vote in **multiple different elections**
- Each election gets your vote (one vote per election)
- **Example**: Vote in Election A, then later vote in Election B

#### If Policy is: üõë RESTRICT to Single Election
- You can vote in **only ONE election**
- Once you vote anywhere, you're done
- **Example**: Vote in Election A, then CANNOT vote in Election B

### Voting Steps
1. Log in with your voter ID
2. You'll see available elections/candidates
3. Select your choice
4. Confirm your vote
5. Vote is recorded

### If You Can't Vote
**Message**: "You have already voted!"

**Why?** Check with admin - either:
- You already voted in that election, OR
- Single-election policy is active and you voted elsewhere

**What to Do**: 
- View your voting history in the app
- Contact election administrator if confused

## Troubleshooting üîß

### "I can't vote in the second election"
**Check**: 
- Is voting allowed? (Ask admin if elections are open)
- Did you vote before? (Check history on login screen)
- What's the policy? (ALLOW=ok, RESTRICT=blocked)

### "I forgot what elections I voted in"
**Solution**:
1. Login to voting interface
2. View "Your Voting History" 
3. See all elections you participated in

### "Admin changed policy and now I'm blocked"
**Note**: Policy change applies to NEW votes only
- If you already voted, you're recorded
- Contact admin if there's an issue
